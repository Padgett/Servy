About Us

Welcome to Wildthings Animal Rescue! We are a non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing and rehabilitating injured, orphaned, and abandoned wildlife. Our mission is to provide a safe haven for animals in need and to promote the conservation of wildlife through education and community outreach.

Founded in 2010, Wildthings Animal Rescue has helped thousands of animals return to their natural habitats. Our team of dedicated volunteers and professionals work tirelessly to ensure that each animal receives the care and attention they need to thrive.

We believe that every animal deserves a chance at a healthy and happy life. Thank you for supporting our mission and helping us make a difference in the lives of wild animals.
